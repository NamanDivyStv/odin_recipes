Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Egg Fried Rice is a quick and flavorful dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with scrambled eggs, vegetables, and seasonings like soy sauce and pepper. Often enjoyed as a standalone meal or a side dish, it's a popular choice in Chinese cuisine and widely loved for its simplicity and adaptability. The dish typically includes ingredients like green onions, carrots, and peas, and can be customized with proteins like chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Lightly seasoned and cooked on high heat, egg fried rice delivers a perfect balance of savory taste and comforting texture.

Ingredient Required

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold, day-old rice)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or sesame oil)
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 2–3 green onions (spring onions), chopped
  • 1–2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon sesame oil (optional, for extra flavor)

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them until fully cooked. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the remaining oil and sauté the garlic and onions until fragrant and softened.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes until tender.
  5. Add the cold, cooked rice to the pan, breaking apart any clumps. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce, and season with salt and pepper. Stir well to coat the rice evenly.
  7. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix them into the rice.
  8. Add green onions and sesame oil (if using), and stir everything together. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your egg fried rice!
